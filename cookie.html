Privacy policy on cookies usage

Your agreement

If you continue to use our website that means you agree to our placing cookies on your computer so as to analyze the way you use our website. Please read this Cookie Policy closely for detailed information what we collect you use this website.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small file that includes letters and numbers that are placed on your browser or your computer. The information that cookies contain is transferred to your computer. In addition, there are two kinds of cookies:

“Persistent cookies” - remain on a user’s device for the specified in the cookie period, and they are activated each time the user opens website.

"Session cookies" - these are temporarily stored on a user’s device; these cookies are deleted once you close the browser.

First | third-party cookies

Both first and third party cookies are used on our website. Whether a cookie is 'first' or 'third' party refers to the domain placing the cookie. First-party cookies are placed by a website the user visits at the time. Third-party cookies are cookies that are set by a domain other than that of the website being visited by the user.

What kind of cookies do we use and why?

Strictly Necessary, Functionality, Performance, and Targeting cookies are used by our website.

Strictly Necessary: let you move around the website and use various necessary features. These cookies are one of the conditions of using our website and services. We cannot provide services you have asked for. These cookies are used to save information you have entered in forms during a browser session; to identify you when you are logged in to our website; and to route users to specific applications of a service, or specific servers.

Functionality: these cookies are used to provide services or retain settings to improve your website session. They are used remember settings you have applied.

Performance: cookies that are needed to save information about how you use our website. They do not collect identification info. We use Performance cookies to put together statistics on how our website is being used; to see how effective our adverts are, including those on third party sites etc.

Targeting: connected that third party cookies provide. Third parties provide these services in return for recognizing that you have already visited our website. Moreover, targeting cookies are used to provide advertising agencies with information on your visit so that they can present you with advertisements in which you may be interested.
